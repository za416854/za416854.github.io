03 : About me

About myself

Hi, my name is Kris Hu, and I'm a software engineer with experience in both personal projects and professional work. During my 6-month bootcamp in Taiwan, I gained a solid foundation in the .NET framework for back-end development, as well as front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, and Vue.js.

In my professional experience, I have worked on a variety of projects, including the Legal Person Property Insurance project and the Taiwan Life Real Estate Management System project. In these projects, I utilized Angular.js and advanced technologies like NgRx and RxJS to create dynamic views and ensure efficient rendering. I also implemented RESTful APIs using .NET WebAPI to interact with databases and retrieve necessary data for front-end development.

I have a strong understanding of several common front-end technologies like TypeScript, Webpack, and component lifecycle hooks, and I have used development tools like Visual Studio Code, Angular CLI, and Git. In all my work, I value collaboration with cross-functional teams and effective communication of project progress and issues to ensure timely delivery of high-quality code.
